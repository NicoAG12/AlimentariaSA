ABOUT US


We are a company dedicated to exporting essential foodstuffs for the basis of a healthy life. We seek to develop relationships that stimulate the access to natural food, connecting their nutritional benefits to the interests of the population.

We have a team of passionate professionals with a clear objective: to work responsibly and to develop long-term profitable relationships that help us growth together.

HOW WE WORK





Logistics

We are committed to doing coordinated and responsible work that guarantees us success in all the stages of the process. We have more than 20 years of experience, which gives us the necessary knowledge to maximize the right decisions.



Countries to which we export

We export our product mainly to the following countries: Pakistan, Dubai, Israel, Brazil, The Netherlands, Costa Rica, the Philippines, Chile, and Vietnam.



Quality of our product

Our production meets certified standards to deliver the best quality in our products. We boost controls in every stage in the chain of production and we respect regulations from a productive, environmental, corporate and social perspective.

We are located in Argentina, in the province of Tucuman
